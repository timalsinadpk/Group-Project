    Bhaktapur was the capital of Nepal during the Malla regime which includes old palace, temples & very unique cultural preservations within it. All monuments consist of various types of stone carving & wood carving which is adds beauty in monuments. A short walk around Bhaktapur Durbar Square will help you understand the beauty of the old city & you can taste a famous yogurt 'JuJudhau' which is a special curd made in wooden plates.
    Bhaktapur consists of 2 world heritage sites listed by UNESCO;
    Bhaktapur Durbar Square
    Changunarayan Temple
