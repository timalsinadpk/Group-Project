We rent and sell cultural dresses of Nepal of your choice. You will experience a unique clothing occurence that makes you part of one of different cultural parts of Nepal.We are connected to the best food destinations to make travelers enjoy new places and new taste.
We organize wild honey hunting yearly in rocky hills; in season "April/May" and "November/December." This will be an once in a life time experience for any person who wishes to join wild honey hunting.
We support "Home Stay" business to promote distinct ethnic group of Nepal by engaging tourists in different Nepali household and highly recommend our customers to have experience memorable Nepali Style living. Grab oppurtunity to be closer to the culture and be involved in daily activities of typical household of distinct ethnic groups.
We organize events for you to enjoy some typical festivals of Nepal if it lies in your stay period.
