    Lumbini is Birthplace of Gautam Buddha & is one of the holiest places to Buddhists. Many monuments, monasteries, natural sceneries add beauty to Lumbini. Lumbini is also one of the world heritage sites listed by UNESCO. Different Buddhist organizations from various country have funded to build monasteries & monuments in Lumbini.
    Main Attraction:
    Ashoka Pillar
    Mayadevi Temple
    Monuments & Monastries
